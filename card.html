Koala

During the day Koalas doze, tucked into forks or nooks in the trees, sleeping for up to 18 hours!!

Scientific Name: Phascolarctos cinereus
Average Length: 2-3 feet
Lifespan: 13-18 years in the wild
Tropical Level: Herbivorous

The koala is related to the kangaroo and the wombat. The koala is a marsupial mammal. The reason the koala is called a koala bear is because the koala looks like a teddy bear.
